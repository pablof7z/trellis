What am I looking at?

Both sides receive the same room joins, follow changes, permission revokes, workspace switches, and network reconnects. The left side opens and closes attachment streams from individual event handlers. That is representative code, not a deliberately broken toy: the bug appears when handlers interleave and one close path does not run.

Trellis derives the desired attachment set from canonical input, diffs that collection, and returns open/close commands. Click a stream to read the receipt for why Trellis owns it or why it refuses to keep it open.
